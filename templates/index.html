Prediction of PM 2.5

The purpose of this project was to analyze the historical data of PM 2.5 and give predictions.

After assembling the dummy dataset, we used matplotlib to plot various different durations for PM 2.5 prediction. Factors we looked at included: Within 7 days, Within 15 days, within 30 days and within 60 days. This site provides the source data and visualizations created as part of the analysis, as well as explanations and descriptions of any trends and correlations witnessed.
